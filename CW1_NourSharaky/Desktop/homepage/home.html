Home
Notetaking is an essential learning skill in college and in the workplace. Your memory can take in and hold information only until new information comes along to replace it. This means that meaningful, long term learning doesn’t occur in classes, but after and between classes as you study. Carefully organized notes give you a lasting record of a class and the opportunity to understand and learn what you have heard.

There’s a correlation between taking notes and creative thinking. Creativity isn’t a mythical power, it’s about having lots of ideas and melding the best ones together.

Where do you record and flesh-out these ideas? Notes.

Both Richard Branson and Bill Gates, notable creative thinkers, are avid note takers. Branson offers a clear instruction, “It doesn’t matter how you record your notes — as long as you do.”

This is where a good note taking app comes in hand and you've come to the right place!


About Us


My Sticky Notes is a simple note taking application developed and adapted by a university student for university students to cover all their needs when it comes to easily taking neat and efficient notes.

My Sticky Notes grants students the opportunity to add and modify quick notes and to do lists in order to facilitate their learning and studying experience.

My Sticky Notes’ mission is to help you manage your information more easily. Notes, reminders, lists….. My Sticky Notes helps you keep track of all of these visually on a single online canvas instead of notes stuck to your computer, desk or fridge.

My Sticky Notes is perfect for individuals looking to access their notes anywhere. Best yet, it’s amazingly simple. You’ll be creating notes in seconds.
